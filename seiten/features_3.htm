Three totally different sides take part in the conflict. This means 3 strategies, 3 interfaces, 3 music soundtracks. In short - three games in one!

Multiplayer mode supports up to 15 players: engage in Internet or LAN matches, or access the best-scorer list and the chatrooms any time.

Easy-to-use map editor: in just a few seconds you can create your own mission map and post it on the Internet for immediate download. TopWare Interactive will also make a steady stream of new maps, troops and other surprises available on the Internet.

Handy 3D camera system: Zoom and rotate the camera angle any way you wish, and use the sophisticated tracking system to keep your most critical troops in sight at all times. You can even play with three camera windows simultaneously!
