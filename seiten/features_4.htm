Your troops increase their experience and intelligence from mission to mission. Name your favorite units and design your own battle flags.

Construct your own units from numerous chassis and weapon systems, then go after those upgrades!

Wage battle on, above and below the Earth's surface - on land, in the air, and on the water. The arsenal at your disposal includes planes, tanks, robots, ships, submarines, helicopters and much, much more!

RELEASE: Sept./Oct. 1999
