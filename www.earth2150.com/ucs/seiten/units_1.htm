The main UCS formations are comprised of battle robots with a body mounted on two legs. A new stepping machinery has increased their mobility speed in difficult terrain like sand and mountains, while decreasing the robots' weight and size. This is really vital for unit transportation.

Many years of intensive research into anti-gravity enabled the UCS engineers to construct flying vehicles. A generator device is attached to a light unit, giving it incredible speed and maneuverability. A G-load problem does not exist, as the generator is able to create its own gravity around the unit. This keeps the unit stable and balanced. The generators are normally used with Gargoil-type fighter units, heavy bombers and transporters.
