Weaponry

In 2150, the LC had no army, since it had never been involved in a war. So conventional weapons production had to start in a hurry. The weapons produced were assigned to units who hardly knew how to use them! It soon became painfully clear that rifles and rockets just wouldn't do against an experienced enemy. So the LC scientists started coming up with advanced forms of weaponry. Their first success was the creation of a sonic gun, based on components from the drilling rigs.

Another giant step was the development of an electric shock gun, which can destroy enemy units' metal compounds. More research led to the development of a cloud cover control system, used in offenses against enemy bases. Then came something truly ingenious - a meteorite shower control system for bombing enemy bases from space!
