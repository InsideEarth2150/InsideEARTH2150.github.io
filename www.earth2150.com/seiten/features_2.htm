Ever-changing weather effects: just when you think you've planned everything just right, strong winds force your jets to make emergency landings; rainstorms cause water levels to rise, obliterating landing craft; a blizzard covers buildings and vegetation in a blanket of snow; and meteorite showers lay waste to large sections of territory. Time to rethink your strategy!


Simple to use troop management system and platoon base control: create your own troop formations, change the squads and patrol areas as necessary, and enlist the aid of computer generals in perfecting your campaign.

Over 50 in-depth video sequences, tightly integrated into the game, will provide you with vital information, including follow-up reports, weather forecasts and much, much more.
